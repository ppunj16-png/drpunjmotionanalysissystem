Patient Positioning
📷 Camera guide loading…
📏 Step 1 — Camera DistancePlace camera at 1.5–2.0 metres from patient. Patient should fit fully in frame head to toe with ~10cm margin top and bottom.
👤 Step 2 — Patient PositionPatient stands in relaxed anatomical position. Arms by sides. Feet shoulder-width. Eyes forward. Light fitted clothing. Remove shoes.
📐 Step 3 — Camera HeightCamera at mid-torso height (approx. umbilicus level). Keep camera level — do not tilt up or down. Use a tripod or steady surface.
💡 Step 4 — LightingEnsure even lighting from front. Avoid strong backlighting from windows. Contrast clothing against background helps detection.
